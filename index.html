Closet-X

Closet-X is a digital closet that allows users to upload items and combine them into outfits. (Collaboration with multiple contributors)
Features:

  • Built with MongoDB, Express, React/Redux, Node, and AWS.
  • React DnD (Drag and Drop) incorporated to allow users to drag their items onto a canvas where they can be arranged as desired.
  • Item upload is simple and quick. Upload any photo to add it to your closet.
